The SaaSNova Story

Why execution, not advice, is the only thing that produces pipeline.

"Every ISV I worked with faced the same pattern: listed on AWS, Azure, or GCP; submissions filed; cloud field teams unresponsive; and nothing attributable showing up at the board level. I fell in love with this problem because it was universal and absolutely solvable. SaaSNova exists because this isn’t a marketplace problem. It’s an execution problem across the entire cloud GTM motion."

Jen Dawson, Founder & CEO, SaaSNova

After years embedded in cloud ecosystems, building marketplace programs on AWS, managing ISV Accelerate motions, and developing the field relationships that drive momentum on Azure and GCP, Jen founded SaaSNova to fill a gap no platform or consultancy was filling: operator-led execution.

Platforms give ISVs listing infrastructure. Consultancies give ISVs frameworks. Neither drives field enablement, activates partner programs, or unlocks marketplace momentum. SaaSNova does all of it, directly, as an embedded operator with clear deliverables in every program period.

  • Execution, not advisory. Every program has named, documented deliverables, not recommendations.
  • True multi-cloud depth. AWS, Azure, and GCP are equally supported, not afterthoughts.
  • Board-visible output. Every program produces a clear pipeline report boards and investors can present directly.
