A Different Approach to Third-Party Risk

Traditional TPRM runs on manual questionnaires, spreadsheets, and start-and-stop workflows. SAFE TPRM replaces all of it. Agentic AI automates the entire vendor risk lifecycle at enterprise scale, the first fully autonomous TPRM solution available today.

The results are fewer blind spots, faster vendor decisions, dramatically less manual work, and a strategic shift from reactive compliance to proactive resilience. For security and risk teams, this means less time navigating process and more time reducing actual risk.

The SAFE TPRM Advantage

With SAFE TPRM, organizations can scale their TPRM program without adding headcount, assess third parties based on true loss exposure in terms acceptable to regulators and auditors, and automate onboarding, risk assessment, continuous monitoring, and regulatory reporting end to end.
